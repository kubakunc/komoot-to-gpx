Privacy Policy — Export GPX for Komoot

Last updated: 2026-06-07 · Publisher: VeloLogic Labs · Contact: contact@velologic-labs.eu

1. What we collect

The app collects no account data and has no backend server of its own. It does not have a backend server. All Komoot tour data fetched while you use the app stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Google AdMob, which serves ads inside the app, collects the Advertising ID, your IP address, and basic device / ad performance signals on our behalf, in line with Google's Privacy & Terms .

2. What we don't collect

  • Your name, email, or Komoot password (the password is entered only on Komoot's own login page inside an in-app browser).
  • Your GPS location.
  • Your contacts, photos, or any device storage outside files you explicitly export.
  • Analytics events sent to us (no Firebase Analytics, no PostHog, no custom tracking).

3. Where your Komoot session lives

After you sign in, the app keeps a Komoot session token on your device, encrypted using Android Keystore via Capacitor Preferences. Signing out or clearing the app's data removes it.

4. Network connections

The app communicates with the following endpoints, and only these:

  • api.komoot.de — fetching your tours and GPX coordinates.
  • www.komoot.com — the in-app sign-in page.
  • tile.openstreetmap.org — map tiles for the tour preview.
  • AdMob ad-serving domains operated by Google (googleads.g.doubleclick.net and related).

5. Consent (EEA / UK / Switzerland)

On first launch in covered regions the app shows Google's consent dialog (UMP). If you decline personalized advertising, AdMob serves only non-personalized ads.

To reset consent, go to Android Settings → Apps → Export GPX for Komoot → Storage → Clear storage. The consent dialog will appear again on the next launch.

6. Komoot

"Komoot" is a trademark of komoot GmbH. This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by komoot GmbH. We are an independent third party that uses your own Komoot credentials, entered on Komoot's official login page, to download your tour data on your behalf.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last updated" date. The link to this page is included in the Play Store listing.

9. Analytics & crash reporting

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand how the app is used (screens viewed, exports completed, feature usage) and to receive crash reports. This data covers app interactions, diagnostics, crash logs and a device identifier; it is not used for advertising, and Firebase ad signals are disabled. In the EEA, analytics identifiers are only enabled after the consent dialog is resolved. Data is processed by Google LLC under Google's privacy policy.

10. Contact

Questions or removal requests: contact@velologic-labs.eu.
