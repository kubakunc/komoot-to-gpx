Export Komoot tours as GPX

Download your own Komoot routes — including private ones — as standard GPX files for your Garmin, Wahoo, bike computer or GPS watch. Free Android app.

How it works

1. Sign in with KomootA secure Komoot login page opens inside the app — we never see your password.
2. Pick a tourBrowse every recorded and planned route, or share a tour from the Komoot app (Share → Export GPX).
3. Save the GPXOne tap saves a standard GPX 1.1 file anywhere on your phone, ready for any device.

Frequently asked questions

How do I export a GPX file from Komoot?

Install Export GPX for Komoot, sign in with your Komoot account, open any tour and tap GPX. The file is saved wherever you choose — Downloads, Google Drive or Dropbox.

Does it work with private tours?

Yes. You sign in with your own account, so the app sees exactly the tours you see — recorded and planned, public and private.

Which devices can use the GPX file?

Any device or app that imports GPX: Garmin Connect, Wahoo, Hammerhead, Bryton, Coros, Suunto, OsmAnd, Locus Map, Gaia GPS and more.

Is it free?

Yes — the app is free and shows a small banner ad.
